Who We Are

ClearSide Exteriors, LLC

ClearSide Exteriors was founded by a student at Glenbrook South High School who saw an opportunity to serve his community while learning the value of building something from scratch. What started as a summer hustle with a bucket and a squeegee has grown into a trusted local business with a reputation built on quality, reliability, and genuine care for every customer.

From day one, the mission has been simple: show up, do exceptional work, and treat every home and business like it's our own. No cutting corners, no generic results — just the kind of attention to detail that keeps customers coming back season after season.

  • Founded by a Glenbrook South High School student
  • Based on the North Shore, serving Cook County
  • 3+ years in business and growing
  • Locally owned, community focused
“
I started ClearSide because I wanted to build something real — not just a summer job, but a business I was proud of. Three years in, every clean window and happy customer still means just as much as the first one. The North Shore is my community, and this is my way of showing up for it.
— Founder, ClearSide Exteriors, LLC  |  Glenbrook South High School
